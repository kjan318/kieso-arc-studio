Diagnostic: Why is Remote Employee Engagement Dropping?

  • 1.
    Lack of Connection: Engagement survey comments from remote employees are 3x more likely to mention feeling "disconnected" from their team and company culture.
  • 2.
    Career Growth Concerns: Remote employees score 15 points lower on survey items related to "fairness of promotion opportunities" compared to their in-office peers.
  • 3.
    Meeting Fatigue: Collaboration tool data shows remote employees are in 30% more virtual meetings, correlating with lower scores on work-life balance.
